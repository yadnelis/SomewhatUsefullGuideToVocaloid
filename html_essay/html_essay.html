WHAT IS VOCALOID?

What is VOCALOID? It’s a voice synthesizing application. You can purchase different libraries (voicebanks) and tune them to make music with. It’s like text to speech, but it can sing and it’s a lot more complicated… The first version was released commercially in 2004. Currently there’s 5 versions. But that’s not too important unless you’re interested on creating your own music.

The community refers to all the digital singers as VOCALOIDs,however, they also have individual names, for example Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin, Megurine Luka, GUMI, etc. And a good amount has mascots. If you want to know numbers, there’s about 68(public) currently of them according to the Vocaloid Wiki.
